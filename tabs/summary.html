Revision Summary

  • Primary Functions

    1. Completes the electrical circuit by allowing ions to flow.
    2. Maintains electrical neutrality in both half-cells.

  • Direction of Flow

    Anions (negative ions) migrate towards the Anode.
    Cations (positive ions) migrate towards the Cathode.

  • No Electrons!

    Electrons do NOT travel through the salt bridge. They only travel through wires.

  • Choice of Salt

    The electrolyte must be inert. \( KNO_3 \) or \( NaNO_3 \) are universally good choices because they do not form precipitates with heavy metal ions like \( Ag^+ \) or \( Pb^{2+} \).
